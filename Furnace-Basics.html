Tracker Specific Tips




These will basically be tool specific even compared to my beepbox suggestions, there is very little overlap.





Having a midi-capable instrument to connect would be a nice boon, but it's not a requirement.


Making melodies is going to feel beyond granular. Changing the 'step' between notes is the on way to make different note lengths, and it's all segmented across different FM or bit channels.


There's plenty of tutorial content out there for more than just Furnace, but open source tools like it typically see a lot of support in forums and such.
