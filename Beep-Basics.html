Beepbox Specific Tips




I feel like the best way to get you familiarized would be to look at an already completed project.


As such, here is the jummbus page for my project Murmured Echoes.


Once you click edit in the bottom right of the screen, you'll be taken to a screen like this:




After you read the brief control scheme at the bottom, just try putting things down, removing notes, switching instruments, anything really.
