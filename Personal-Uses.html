"So what do I do with this stuff?"


Really, it boils down to a couple things:




1. I like the sound, honestly


Simply put, a lot of combinations and mixes I like only existed in my head for the longest time.


Once I got it in my head to actually do something with them, I found that I generally end up making a lot of loops or background themes. The informal categories were quite handy for that back then.




2. Handy idle audio


As a DM for a tabletop group, I've found a surprising amount of use from having instruments on standby. With enough elbow grease and description, you can sell any random sound as anything.


Combined with free tools like Dungeon Scrawl (a mapping tool), both tools have added a lot of depth..







Couple those reasons with the nascent hope that I could more serious with piano, and it's been a lot of fun self learning. Even if it is tooth grinding at times, haha.
