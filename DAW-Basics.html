D.A.W Recommendations




I'd honestly say to stay away from these for the most part if you're unfamiliar with them.


There is one I would recommend to starters, but that in of itself would need a recommendation to read up on theory from places like musictheory.net.


Musescore isn't a Digital Audio Workstation in the strictest sense, but as a composition tool it has the best compromise between quality and ease of use for learning.





There's no amount of text I could put here to get you prepared for Musescore, so the next best thing I could do would be linking you to their youtube tutorials.
